Photometer - Account and Data Deletion

Developer: Przemysław Pardel

How to delete your account

This page explains how users of the Photometer app can request deletion of their account and associated data.

To request account deletion, please send an email to: kmkmagdziarz@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your Photometer account. Your request will be processed manually by Przemysław Pardel or Kamil Magdziarz.

Data that will be deleted

  • User email address
  • Measurement data (valueAverage, valueMin, valueMax, valueMedian, valueSample, valueDate, valueName)
  • Account information (including Pro status if applicable)

Data retention

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

Processing time

All deletion requests are processed within 7 days.
