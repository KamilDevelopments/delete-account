Account and Data Deletion

How to delete your account

Currently, account deletion is not available directly in the application.

To request account deletion, please send an email to: kmkmagdziarz@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your account. Your request will be processed manually by the administrator.

What data is deleted

  • Email address
  • Measurement data (valueAverage, valueMin, valueMax, valueMedian, valueSample, valueDate, valueName)
  • Account status (e.g. Pro version)

Data retention

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for security and legal reasons.

Processing time

Requests are processed within 7 days.
